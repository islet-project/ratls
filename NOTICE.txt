- License: Apache License, Version 2.0
- License URL: http://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0

As requested by the model publishers, please cite the foundational BERT paper
for the model architecture and pre-processing methodology:

[1] Jacob Devlin, Ming-Wei Chang, Kenton Lee, Kristina Toutanova.
"BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding."
arXiv preprint arXiv:1810.04805, 2018.

For accurate academic attribution of the model compression, please cite:

[2] Zhiqing Sun, Hongkun Yu, Xiaodan Song, Renjie Liu, Yiming Yang, and Denny Zhou.
"MobileBERT: a Compact Task-Agnostic BERT for Resource-Limited Devices."
Proceedings of the 58th Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics. 2020.
arXiv:2004.02984 [cs.CL].
